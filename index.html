About Me

My first office space job was through Habitat for Humanity in Americus, GA (a little town about 3 hours South of Atlanta). This is where my passion for creating begins to emerge. Creating what?? Anything. I love volunteering on build sites, brainstorming innovative ideas, or even constructing team spirit. After a couple years of marriage my wife and I decided to make the move to Atlanta, and I realized I didn't want to stop this passion inside me. I pursued it through a more direct path in the construction world, specifically remodeling and renovation alongside Nelson Exteriors. My eyes were opened to a whole new world of taking older homes with inferior materials and remodeling them to work more efficiently with the modern-day materials available to us today. I built my knowledge from the ground up. It started with an office job to help me leran of the newest and trusted materials we employ, and moved to an in the field project manager role. In this hefty set of responsibility I was reliable for: having materials at the project, crews coordinated for working, and being the main contact for the customer.

Interests I picked up through work:

  • Working hard not for just my own good, but for others as well.
  • Passion for problem solving and streamlining solutions.
  • A keen eye for the technical side of how things operate.
  • An excitement for learning anything I set my mind to.
